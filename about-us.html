Who We Are

Peerali Law is a boutique law firm in Los Angeles specializing in complex and catastrophic injuries. The firm was founded by Kristopher Peerali and Serena Peerali, who are both experienced personal injury attorneys.

Since 2021, they have recovered over $50 million in compensation for clients.

We handle a wide range of personal injury cases, including catastrophic injuries, wrongful death, medical malpractice, funeral home negligence, car accidents and more. No matter how complex or sensitive the situation, we approach every case with determination, compassion, and an unwavering focus on results.

Our experience means we handle virtually every type of personal injury claim in Los Angeles.
