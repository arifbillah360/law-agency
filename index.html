Frequently Asked Questions

How much are Peerali Law's legal fees?

Peerali Law works on a contingency fee basis, which means we only get paid if we win your case. There are no upfront costs or fees. Our payment comes as a percentage of your settlement or award. If we don't win, you owe us nothing.

How much are most catastrophic injury settlements?

Catastrophic injury settlements vary widely depending on the severity of injuries, medical expenses, lost wages, and long-term care needs. Our firm has secured settlements ranging from six figures to multiple millions of dollars. We've recovered over $50 million for our clients since 2021. Contact us for a free consultation to understand what your case may be worth.

What qualifies as a catastrophic injury?

A catastrophic injury is one that results in permanent disability or long-term impairment. This includes brain injuries, spinal cord injuries, paralysis, amputation, severe burns, and injuries requiring extensive medical treatment or ongoing care. If your injury has permanently affected your ability to work or live independently, it may qualify as catastrophic.

What do I do if an insurance adjuster calls me?

Do not give a recorded statement or sign any documents without consulting an attorney first. Insurance adjusters may try to minimize your claim or get you to say something that could hurt your case. Politely decline to discuss the accident and contact Peerali Law immediately. We'll handle all communications with insurance companies on your behalf.
